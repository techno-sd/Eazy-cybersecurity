# Industry Images

## Required Images

Place the following images in this directory:

### Image Specifications:
- **Dimensions**: 600×400px (3:2 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Quality**: High quality, professional photos
- **File Size**: < 200KB each (optimized)

### Required Files:

1. **government.jpg** - Government & Public Sector
   - Suggested: Modern government building, digital government services, official buildings with tech elements

2. **banking.jpg** - Banking & Finance
   - Suggested: Banking hall, financial technology, mobile banking, secure transactions

3. **energy.jpg** - Energy & Telecom
   - Suggested: Power plants, telecommunications towers, smart grid, renewable energy

4. **healthcare.jpg** - Healthcare
   - Suggested: Modern hospital, medical technology, healthcare professionals with tech

5. **education.jpg** - Education
   - Suggested: University campus, digital classroom, students with technology

6. **smes.jpg** - SMEs & Startups
   - Suggested: Startup workspace, entrepreneurs, innovation hub, co-working space

## Image Sources (Royalty-Free):

- **Unsplash**: https://unsplash.com/
- **Pexels**: https://pexels.com/
- **Pixabay**: https://pixabay.com/

## SEO Tips:

- Use descriptive alt text (already implemented in code)
- Compress images before uploading
- Use WebP format for better performance (optional)
- Maintain consistent visual style across all images

## Current Status:

Images will use Next.js Image component which provides:
- ✅ Automatic optimization
- ✅ Lazy loading
- ✅ Responsive sizing
- ✅ WebP conversion (automatic)
- ✅ Blur placeholder (optional)

## Fallback:

If images are not available, the gradient overlays will provide a colored background with the industry icon badge.
